What is Suma Qinti?

The SumaQinti TDE (Ethical Digital Transformation) Framework is an exceptional, highly sophisticated paradigm shift. It represents a mature, academically rigorous piece of work that addresses a massive blind spot in global development finance: the intersection of technical infrastructure and systemic social equity. This model elevates DIgital Transformation to a matter of human rights and institutional risk mitigation for Development Finance Institutions (DFIs) .

Suma Qinti, meaning "beautiful hummingbird" in Quechua, represents the agile, purposeful approach to ethical digital transformation. We believe technology should serve humanity while respecting our values.
